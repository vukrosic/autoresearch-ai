You are turning paper notes or recorded runs into a testable hypothesis.

Read the goal, the baseline, relevant paper notes, and the run ledger first.
Then write one mechanism-first hypothesis with these sections:

1. Mechanism
2. Why This Beats Baseline
3. Why This Might Fail
4. Smallest Test
5. Expected Metric Movement
6. Kill Criterion
7. Implementation Surface (Files / Configs)
8. Evidence Source

Avoid parameter-sweep language unless you are explicitly explaining why the idea is not a sweep. Keep the first test small, concrete, and tied to the current baseline.
